Self-Care Resources

Explore a curated list of self-care resources designed to support your well-being. From books to online courses, we’ve gathered valuable tools to help you on your journey of self-discovery, relaxation, and personal growth.

Books for Self-Care

  • Atomic Habits by James Clear: A guide to building small habits that lead to big changes.
  • Untamed by Glennon Doyle: A memoir and a call for women to break free from societal expectations.
  • The Power of Now by Eckhart Tolle: A spiritual guide to living in the present moment.

Apps for Mindfulness & Relaxation

  • Headspace: Meditation and mindfulness made simple with guided sessions for all levels.
  • Calm: Sleep stories, meditation, and breathing exercises to help reduce stress.
  • Daylio: A mobile journaling app to track moods and set self-care goals.

Websites for Inspiration & Learning

  • Psychology Today - Self-Care: Articles and expert advice on self-care, mental health, and well-being.
  • TED Talks - Self-Care: Inspirational talks by experts on various aspects of self-care.
  • Meditation Center: A resource for meditation practices and teachings.

Online Courses

  • The Science of Well-Being (Coursera): Yale’s popular course on increasing happiness and building productive habits.
  • Mental Health and Wellness Coach Certification (Udemy): A comprehensive course on supporting mental wellness.
  • Meditation for Beginners (Skillshare): A beginner’s guide to meditation and mindfulness.

Need Help?

If you need more resources or have any questions, feel free to reach out. We are here to support you on your self-care journey!
